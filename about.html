About Blossom Hill

Blossom Hill Assisted Living - A Place to Flourish, Where Life Blooms

Comprehensive Care in a Wheelchair Accessible Facility

At Blossom Hill, we provide personalized care tailored to meet the individual needs of each resident. Our facility is designed to ensure accessibility for everyone, including those who use wheelchairs. From wide hallways to specially adapted rooms, we’ve created an environment that promotes independence while providing the necessary support and care. We also offer specialized accessibility features to ensure all residents can navigate comfortably.

Explore Blossom Hill Through Our Gallery

We invite you to explore our gallery and see firsthand the welcoming environment at Blossom Hill. You’ll find images of our fully accessible spaces and the compassionate care we provide to our residents. Our goal is to create a comfortable, safe, and engaging atmosphere where residents can truly feel at home.

For more information, check out our FAQ page to find answers to common questions. If you're interested in joining our dedicated team, we invite you to explore our careers page. For further inquiries or to schedule a tour, don’t hesitate to contact us. Our team is ready to assist you!
