A four Minute Walk to Carver Lake Park & Beach

Blossom Hill Assisted Living is conveniently located just a short 4-minute walk from Carver Lake Park and Beach in Woodbury, MN. This scenic park offers a variety of outdoor amenities, including a swimming beach, picnic areas, volleyball courts, and an accessible playground. Residents and visitors can enjoy leisurely walks along the lake, participate in recreational activities, or simply relax in the serene environment. The park's close proximity enhances the quality of life for those at Blossom Hill, providing easy access to nature and outdoor recreation.
