About Blossom Hill

Blossom Hill Assisted Living with Skilled Nursing – A Place to Flourish, Where Life Blooms

Comprehensive Skilled Nursing & Accessible Care

At Blossom Hill, we specialize in skilled nursing and personalized support tailored to each individual. Our wheelchair-accessible facility includes wide hallways, barrier-free spaces, and renovated bathrooms featuring zero-entry roll-in showers, floating vanities, and spacious layouts for ease of use and comfort. From rehabilitation services to daily assistance, our skilled nursing team provides the highest level of care in an inclusive, welcoming environment. Learn more about our accessibility features.

Personalized Comfort & Holistic Well-Being

Beyond exceptional skilled nursing, Blossom Hill is dedicated to creating a warm, home-like atmosphere where every resident feels valued and supported. Our residents enjoy thoughtfully designed private and shared living spaces, nutritious meals tailored to individual needs, and engaging activities that promote mental, emotional, and physical well-being. With a focus on dignity, respect, and individualized care, our team is committed to enhancing quality of life for every person who calls Blossom Hill home.

For more information, visit our FAQ page, explore career opportunities, or contact us to arrange a tour. Our skilled nursing team is here to answer your questions and provide the support you need.

Expert Skilled Nursing Care

At Blossom Hill, we provide a compassionate, professional skilled nursing environment where residents receive the highest level of medical and personal care. Our facility is designed to meet the needs of individuals requiring skilled nursing support, ensuring comfort, safety, and dignity in every aspect of daily living. It's not just about health care—it's about creating a place where each resident feels supported, secure, and truly at home.

At Blossom Hill, we go beyond traditional assisted living by offering skilled nursing care tailored to each resident’s unique health needs. Our highly trained team provides 24/7 medical support, rehabilitation assistance, and hands-on care with compassion and professionalism.. From medication management, wound care and rehabilitation support to assistance with daily activities, our licensed nursing team is committed to maintaining health, comfort, and quality of life. With a focus on professional care delivered with warmth and respect, every day at Blossom Hill is designed to promote healing, comfort, and peace of mind. It's a place where life is nurtured, health is prioritized, and every resident experiences attentive, compassionate care in a safe, welcoming environment.

Personalized Care

Every resident is unique, and so is their care. At Blossom Hill, we believe in celebrating individuality by taking the time to understand each resident's personal story, preferences, and goals. We work closely with residents and their families to develop customized care plans that not only address their needs but also honor their lifestyle and aspirations.

Our compassionate team ensures that every aspect of care is thoughtfully tailored to promote comfort, dignity, and joy. Whether it's through daily assistance, meaningful activities, or simply offering a listening ear, we strive to provide a level of care that goes beyond expectations.

Our focus is on empowering residents to maintain their independence and confidence while offering the support they need to thrive. By creating a harmonious balance between care and autonomy, we enable our residents to lead fulfilling lives in an environment where they feel truly valued and understood.

Medication Management

At Blossom Hill, we understand the importance of proper medication management in maintaining health and well-being. Our dedicated team ensures that each resident’s medications are administered accurately and on time, with careful attention to any changes in prescriptions. We work closely with healthcare providers to monitor health, address any concerns, and provide peace of mind for both residents and their families.

Dedicated Team

Our compassionate caregivers are the heart of Blossom Hill. With years of experience and a deep commitment to the well-being of our residents, our team ensures that each resident receives attentive, respectful, and individualized care. We go above and beyond to create a positive and supportive environment for every resident.

Nutritious Meals

We believe that good nutrition is key to maintaining health and vitality. Our home-cooked meals are not only delicious but also tailored to meet dietary needs and preferences. We focus on providing balanced, nutritious meals that enhance our residents' well-being while catering to specific health requirements.

Peace of Mind

With open communication and 24/7 supervision, families can rest easy knowing their loved ones are in good hands. We understand the importance of trust, which is why we ensure that families are always informed about their loved one’s care and well-being. Our safe, supportive environment gives peace of mind to both residents and families.
